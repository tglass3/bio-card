Talia Cesal

Talia Cesal is a Senior at Eastern Washington University, studying Visual Communication Design. She has graduated from Spokane Falls Community College with an AAS-T in Graphic Design and has been freelancing in illustration since 2015.


Talia has developed a unique illustration style that fuses elements from anime and western cartoons, and strives to achieve a balance between minimalism and maximalism in her design style.
